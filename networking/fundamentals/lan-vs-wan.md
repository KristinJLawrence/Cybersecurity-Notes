# LAN vs WAN

## LAN (Local Area Network)

A LAN connects devices in a limited area such as:

- Homes
- Schools
- Offices

Characteristics:

- High speed
- Low latency
- Privately managed

Examples:
- Home WiFi network
- Office internal network

## WAN (Wide Area Network)

A WAN connects multiple LANs across large geographic areas.

Characteristics:

- Covers cities, countries, or continents
- Often uses public infrastructure

Example:
- The Internet
